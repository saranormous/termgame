The terminal is a text interface that sits underneath your entire computer — every app, file, and internet service is reachable from it. It's where AI coding agents live, because from here they don't just answer questions: they do things.

In five parts you'll install a coding agent, build a Tetris game with it, ship it to the real internet, write a script that organizes your files, see how the terminal connects to everything else on your machine, and learn what to do when things break.

This is a pretend computer inside your browser. Nothing touches your real files. Practice here first, then do it on your machine, for real.
